Regulatory Tripwire Monitor
Legal Exposure Mapped Onto Your Supply Chain Graph

Under 32 CFR § 170.23, primes are required to flow CMMC levels down contractually. If a prime knowingly awards work to a non-compliant subcontractor, it faces False Claims Act liability. Morningstar maps regulatory exposure directly onto the supply chain graph.

  • ITAR/EAR classification propagation: foreign ownership changes trigger automatic program-level alerts
  • CMMC readiness scoring by supplier node with expiration tracking against contract period of performance
  • DCSA/FOCI beneficial ownership monitoring with foreign investment alerts
Legal Moat SAP and Kinaxis explicitly don't touch this — it's too vertical.

Program Schedule Entanglement
Bridging Supply Chain Risk to Program Management

Current SCM tools think in terms of inventory and delivery. Aerospace primes think in terms of Integrated Master Schedules (IMS) and Earned Value Management (EVM). Morningstar bridges these worlds.

  • Ingests MS Project, Primavera P6, or DCMA open milestones
  • Computes which disruptions cause schedule variance triggering DCMA Corrective Action Requests
  • Surface "program-level blast radius": one supplier failure → affected CLINs → deliverables → award fees
  • Connects FPDS-NG and USASpending.gov for public contract vehicle data
